2N3055 NPN Power Transistor Details

NPN Power Transistor (2N3055)

Specifications:

  • Type: NPN Power Transistor
  • Collector-Emitter Voltage (VCEO): 60V
  • Continuous Collector Current (IC): 15A
  • DC Current Gain (hFE): 20 - 70
  • Package: TO-3
  • Operating Temperature: -55°C to +150°C

Applications: Power Amplifiers, Switching Regulators, Motor Control

Military and Industrial Standards: Meets MIL-STD-1234 requirements.

Packaging Types: Individual packaging for manual assembly.
