2N2222A NPN Power Transistor Details

NPN Power Transistor (2N2222A)

Specifications:

  • Type: NPN Power Transistor
  • Collector-Emitter Voltage (VCEO): 40V
  • Continuous Collector Current (IC): 800mA
  • DC Current Gain (hFE): 100 - 300
  • Package: TO-92
  • Operating Temperature: -40°C to +175°C

Applications: Power Switching, Audio Amplifiers, Motor Drivers

Military and Industrial Standards: Meets MIL-STD-1234 requirements.

Packaging Types: Tube packaging for manual assembly.
