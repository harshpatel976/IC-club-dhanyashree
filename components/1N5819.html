1N5819 Schottky Diode Details

Schottky Rectifier Diode (1N5819)

Specifications:

  • Type: Schottky Rectifier
  • Maximum Repetitive Peak Reverse Voltage: 40V
  • Maximum RMS Voltage: 28V
  • Maximum DC Blocking Voltage: 40V
  • Maximum Average Forward Rectified Current: 1A
  • Package: DO-41 (Through-Hole)
  • Forward Voltage: 0.45V @ 1A
  • Recovery Time: 200 ns

Applications: Low-Voltage Rectification, High-Speed Switching, Reverse Current Protection

Military and Industrial Standards: Meets MIL-STD-1234 requirements.

Packaging Types: Tube packaging for manual assembly.
