IRF540N Power N-Channel MOSFET Details

Power N-Channel MOSFET (IRF540N)

Specifications:

  • Type: Power N-Channel MOSFET
  • Drain-Source Voltage (VDS): 100V
  • Continuous Drain Current (ID): 33A
  • Gate Threshold Voltage (VGS(th)): 2V - 4V
  • RDS(on): 0.044Ω @ VGS = 10V
  • Package: TO-220
  • Operating Temperature: -40°C to +175°C

Applications: Power Supplies, Motor Drivers, Audio Amplifiers

Military and Industrial Standards: Meets MIL-STD-1234 requirements.

Packaging Types: Tube packaging for manual assembly.
